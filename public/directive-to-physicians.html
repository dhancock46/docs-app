Full Legal Name of Principal

Use your full legal name such as the name on your drivers license or passport. Include any suffixes such as "Jr." or "Sr." Do not use prefixes such as "Mr." or "Ms.". Do not use nicknames such as "Billy" rather than "William."

Primary Healthcare Decision Agent

Use the full legal name of the person you trust to make healthcare decisions for you when you cannot make them yourself. Include any suffixes such as "Jr." or "Sr." Do not use prefixes such as "Mr." or "Ms.". Do not use nicknames.

Alternate Agents

You are not required to name an alternate agent. However, if you have someone you trust to make healthcare decisions, naming an alternate will help ensure someone can make healthcare decisions for you if your primary agent is unavailable when needed.

Date the Document Will Be Signed

Do not guess the date the document will be signed. If you are not sure of the date, select the "I don't know the exact signing date" option. If you know the month but not the date you can add the month and leave the day blank to be filled in at signing or you can leave the entire date blank.
